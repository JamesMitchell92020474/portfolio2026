About

I design things and build them.

I started out in graphic design — brand work, print production, and the kind of detail-obsessed discipline that comes with pre-press. Over time I moved into IT systems and administration, and then into front-end development. The combination makes me more useful, not harder to categorise.

On the design side I have extensive experience with Adobe CC across InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator. I've worked on brand design, print production, and pre-press for small businesses and in-house teams — the full lifecycle from brief to finished file.

In development I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have two years of experience on the JADE platform including bug fixing, feature development, unit testing, peer review, and documentation, alongside systems administration experience managing users, CRM integrations, and internal tooling.

I'm currently looking for permanent full-time roles in front-end development, UI/UX, or IT — in Christchurch or remotely.
