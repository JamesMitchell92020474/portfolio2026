About

Design thinking.
Development skills.

I started out in graphic design — brand work, print production, and the kind of detail-obsessed discipline that comes with pre-press. Over time I moved into IT systems and administration, which gave me a different lens: how organisations work, how software fits into workflows, and how users actually interact with the things we build. From there, front-end development was a natural step.

That combination — design, systems, and code — means I can work across the full width of a project. I understand what a design needs to communicate and I know how to build it. I don't need a handoff to get from a brief to a finished, working page.

On the design side I work primarily in Adobe CC across brand identity, print collateral, and pre-press. On the development side I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with version control via GitHub. I'm currently looking for permanent full-time roles in front-end development, UI/UX, or IT — in Christchurch or remotely.
