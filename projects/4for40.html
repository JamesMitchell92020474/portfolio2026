Islam Is… 4 for 40

Islam Is… 4 for 40 is a Christchurch-based educational programme built around a simple, powerful idea: four minutes of reading a day, for 40 days, exploring Islamic culture, history, and beliefs. My involvement has included extending their existing website — adding a new page originally built in HTML and CSS, then refactored into React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS to match the existing codebase — via GitHub, designing print flyers, and formatting and publishing content to their Substack.

I've also supported the team operationally: setting up hardware, migrating email between platforms, extracting and reporting on learner data from their LMS, and keeping meeting records via Fireflies. It's a role that draws on the full range of my skills, and one I find genuinely worthwhile — the programme does real, meaningful work in the community.
